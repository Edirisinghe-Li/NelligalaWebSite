Feel The Beauty Of The Nelligala


The Nelligala International Buddhist Center is a beautiful modern Buddhist temple that is situated at the top of the Nelligala Mountain, Muruthalawa village in Kandy District, Central Province in Sri Lanka. Nelligala temple is still under construction and its construction was started in 2015. Reaching the top of Nelligala one can experience the most amazing views which could sometimes be covered by the ongoing mist. However, once the mist clears off the view can be the most beautiful mountain landscape that you've ever seen. On a clear day, viewers can see Adam’s Peak. If one looks at the direction of Sumana Saman Devi’s statue, you will be able to see Adam’s Peak.

Most Popular Sights In Nelligala Temple


Nelligala International Buddhist Center is famous among local and foreign tourists for its beautiful landscapes and visited by both Buddhists and people from various other religions. It is a place which brings along peace and tranquility to its visitors. The gold color Buddha statues, golden stupa of the temple and Sumana Saman God’s statue creates a unique and picturesque sight to see in contrast with the blue sky above. The temple includes sacred relics of Lord Buddha and one can see their clear view in Relic chamber.
